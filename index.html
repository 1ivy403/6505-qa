Concepts & mechanisms概念与机制类
C
What exactly is "shadow price"? How is it different from the actual carbon tax? 「影子价格」到底是什么？和实际缴纳的碳税有什么不同？
›
Shadow Price ICP Desjardins 2024
1
Shadow price = company's predicted future carbon cost, not today's bill. It includes compliance spending, quota purchase, and internal pricing. 影子价格 = 企业对未来碳成本的预期，不是今天账单；含合规支出、配额购买与内部定价。
2
Carbon tax = external, mandatory, government-set. Shadow price = internal, voluntary, forward-looking. 碳税 = 外部、强制、政府定价；影子价格 = 内部、自愿、面向未来。
3
The key concern: will long-term cash flow survive policy tightening? Will assets become stranded? 核心关切：政策收紧后长期现金流能否扛住？资产会否搁浅？
Key sentence: "Shadow price is not what you pay today — it's what you expect to pay tomorrow, and that expectation shapes where capital goes."金句（中文）：「影子价格不是今天付多少，而是你预期明天付多少——预期决定资本往哪流。」
C
Can you explain the relationship between external carbon pricing and internal carbon pricing? 外部碳定价与内部碳定价之间是什么关系？
›
External vs Internal Capital Redistribution CDP & World Bank 2025
1
External: government sets the price (carbon tax or ETS) → mandatory for all market participants. 外部：政府定价（碳税或碳市场）→ 对市场主体强制适用。
2
Internal (ICP): company voluntarily sets a virtual carbon price → writes it into project approval → guides internal capital allocation. 内部（ICP）：企业自设内部碳价 → 写入项目审批 → 引导内部资金配置。
3
The logic: external price creates cost signal → company responds with ICP → funds flow from high-carbon to low-carbon projects. 逻辑：外部价格形成成本信号 → 企业用 ICP 响应 → 资金从高碳项目流向低碳项目。
4
Over 1,700 institutions now use ICP globally. BP and Shell both set $40/ton, targeting $100/ton by 2030. 全球已有逾 1700 家机构使用 ICP；BP 与 Shell 均设 40 美元/吨，目标 2030 年约 100 美元/吨。
Key sentence: "External pricing forces the question; internal pricing decides the answer — where exactly does the money go inside the company."金句：「外部定价逼出问题，内部定价决定答案——钱在公司内部到底流向哪里。」
C
How do carbon credits fit into this picture? What about quality concerns? 碳信用在整个框架里起什么作用？质量/「漂绿」风险怎么说？
›
Carbon Credits Greenwashing CFC 2024 / Kita Earth 2023
1
Carbon credits = bridge between ICP and external emission reduction projects. When internal cuts aren't enough, companies buy credits to offset residual emissions. 碳信用是 ICP 与外部减排项目之间的桥梁；内部减不下来时，企业购买信用抵消残余排放。
2
Two demand sources in Canada: compliance demand (OBPS system) + voluntary commitment (net-zero targets). 加拿大两类需求：合规需求（如 OBPS）+ 自愿承诺（净零目标等）。
3
Quality varies widely. Must meet: additionality, permanence, third-party verification. 质量参差；需满足额外性、持久性、第三方核证等。
4
As Canada's price hits $80 CAD/ton, companies prefer high-quality credits → carbon credit insurance is emerging. 加国碳价升至约 80 加元/吨，企业更偏好高质量信用 → 碳信用保险等工具出现。
Key sentence: "The channel matters as much as the price — if credit quality is poor, the capital never actually reaches real emission reduction."金句：「渠道和价格同样重要——信用质量差，资金就到不了真实减排。」
Challenges & pushback质疑与挑战类
!
If carbon pricing is economically efficient, why is there so much political opposition in Canada? 若碳定价经济上有效率，为何加拿大政治反弹仍这么大？
›
Political Backlash Provinces Harrison & Schwarz 2024
1
Carbon price increase + inflation → combined effect makes energy visibly more expensive for voters. 碳价上涨叠通胀 → 选民直观感到能源更贵。
2
Although rebate exists, people feel the cost immediately but receive the rebate later — timing mismatch. 虽有退税，但民众先感到成本、后拿到退税——时间错配。
3
Provincial economic structures differ hugely — resource-dependent provinces (e.g. Alberta) face disproportionate burden. 各省经济结构差异大——资源型省（如阿省）承受不成比例压力。
4
Conservative Party uses it as a political weapon → lack of cross-party consensus makes policy vulnerable to election cycles. 保守党将其政治化 → 缺乏跨党共识使政策易受选举周期冲击。
Key sentence: "Economic efficiency doesn't guarantee political durability. If people feel the cost but can't see the benefit, any carbon price becomes a target."金句：「经济有效率不等于政治扛得住；若只感到成本、看不到收益，任何碳价都会成靶子。」
!
You mentioned policy uncertainty weakens investment. Doesn't that contradict the argument that carbon pricing guides investment? 你们说政策不确定性削弱投资，这和「碳定价引导投资」是否矛盾？
›
CPU Contradiction? Gavriilidis 2026 / Liu 2026
1
No contradiction — it's a tension, not a paradox. Carbon pricing aims to raise expectations; uncertainty dampens the response. 不矛盾——是张力而非悖论：碳定价抬高预期，不确定性压低投资反应。
2
When policy signals are clear and stable → shadow price anchors expectations → investment follows. 信号清晰稳定 → 影子价格锚定预期 → 投资跟进。
3
When signals are blurred (e.g. 2025 benchmark update) → companies apply heavy conservative discounting → investment response weakens. 信号模糊（如 2025 基准线调整）→ 企业大幅保守折现 → 投资反应变弱。
4
This is exactly why our policy recommendations emphasize signal stability and cross-party consensus. 故政策建议强调信号稳定与跨党共识。
Key sentence: "Shadow price is the result of two forces — policy pushing expectations up and uncertainty pulling investment response down. The outcome depends on which force wins."金句：「影子价格是两股力：政策推高预期，不确定性压低投资反应——谁占上风决定结果。」
!
Is the Canada Carbon Rebate actually effective? Does it really offset the cost for households? 加拿大碳退税真的有效吗？能抵消家庭负担吗？
›
Carbon Rebate Household Impact
1
Official data: rebate can offset or even exceed direct carbon costs for most families. 官方数据：对多数家庭退税可抵消甚至超过直接碳成本。
2
But the problem is perception vs. reality: people feel the price rise at the pump immediately; the rebate comes quarterly. 难点在感知与现实：油价上涨立即可感，退税按季到账。
3
Low-income and rural households may still face net burden due to higher energy dependence. 低收入与农村家庭因能源依赖更高仍可能净受损。
4
This is why we recommend prioritizing disadvantaged groups and making revenue use more transparent. 因此建议优先保护弱势群体并提高收入用途透明度。
Key sentence: "The rebate works on paper, but politics runs on perception. If people can't feel the benefit, the policy loses support."金句：「纸面上退税有效，政治上靠感知；感受不到收益，支持度就掉。」
!
You said ICP doesn't push investment equally. Can you explain the "shielding effect"? 你们说 ICP 对投资的推动不均匀，请解释「遮蔽效应」。
›
ICP Shielding Effect Qin 2023
1
Qin (2023) tested ICP's channels using synthetic control method. Qin（2023）用合成控制等方法检验 ICP 传导渠道。
2
Energy intensity = positive mediator: energy-heavy projects respond well to carbon cost signals. 能源强度为正中介：高耗能项目对碳成本信号反应更明显。
3
R&D spending = shielding effect: R&D absorbs the pressure and "blocks" the signal from reaching other investment types. 研发支出呈遮蔽效应：研发「吸收」压力，阻碍信号传导至其他投资类型。
4
Implication: shadow price enters the budget, but internal distribution is uneven — not all departments benefit equally. 含义：影子价格进预算，但内部分配不均——并非各部门同等受益。
Key sentence: "ICP opens the door, but R&D acts like a sponge — it absorbs the carbon cost pressure before it reaches other investment channels."金句：「ICP 开了门，但研发像海绵——在信号到达其他投资渠道前先吸走碳成本压力。」

How does Canada's approach compare with EU ETS and China's carbon market? 加拿大模式与欧盟 ETS、中国碳市场相比如何？
›
EU ETS China Carbon Market World Bank 2025
1
Canada: hybrid model — federal carbon tax (consumer) + baseline-and-credit (industry) + provincial flexibility. 加拿大：混合模式——联邦碳税（消费端）+ 基准线—信用（产业）+ 省级灵活安排。
2
EU ETS: cap-and-trade, market-determined price. Reached €90+/ton early 2026, then fell to €60-70 range. Most mature system globally. 欧盟 ETS：总量交易、价格由市场形成；2026 年初曾达 90+ 欧元/吨后回落至约 60–70 区间，体系最成熟。
3
China: intensity-based design linked to output — better suited for a growing economy. Price is lower but coverage is expanding. 中国：强度型设计与产出挂钩——更适合增长型经济体；价偏低但覆盖在扩。
4
Together, ETS + carbon tax cover about 28% of global GHG emissions. ETS 与碳税合计覆盖约全球 28% 温室气体排放。
Key sentence: "Canada chose price certainty (tax), EU chose quantity certainty (cap), China chose flexibility (intensity-based). Different economies, different designs — but the goal is the same: redirect capital."金句：「加拿大量价确定、欧盟量确定、中国重灵活——国情不同设计不同，目标都是引导资本。」

Policy recommendations政策建议类
R
What are your main policy recommendations? 你们的主要政策建议有哪些？
›
5 Recommendations Harrison 2024 / Rabe 2023 / Jaccard 2024
1
Balance price increase & livelihood: gradual increase, tie revenue to livelihood protection, prioritize low-income groups. 平衡涨价与民生：渐进提价，收入与民生保障挂钩，优先低收入群体。
2
Regional flexibility: national minimum + provincial autonomy, differentiated support for resource-dependent regions. 区域弹性：国家底线 + 省际自主，对资源型地区差异化支持。
3
Diversified policy portfolio: don't rely on carbon tax alone. Build "pricing + incentive + transition support" framework. 政策组合：勿单靠碳税；构建「定价 + 激励 + 转型支持」框架。
4
Cross-party consensus: legally enshrine carbon pricing to reduce vulnerability to election cycles. 跨党共识：法律化制度化碳定价，降低选举周期冲击。
5
Revenue transparency: publicly disclose how carbon revenue is collected, allocated, and spent. 收入透明：公开碳收入的征集、分配与使用。
Key sentence: "The answer to the debate is not 'for or against carbon pricing' — it's how to design a system that is both effective and politically sustainable."金句：「争论不在『要不要碳定价』，而在如何设计既有效又政治可持续的体系。」
R
Is cross-party consensus realistic? How would you actually achieve it? 跨党共识现实吗？具体怎么做到？
›
Cross-party Institutionalization Rabe 2023
1
We acknowledge it's difficult — that's exactly the point. Canada's biggest vulnerability is policy depending on which party is in power. 承认很难——这正是问题：加拿大最大脆弱性是政策随执政党摇摆。
2
Two paths: (a) legal entrenchment — write carbon pricing rules into law so they can't be easily reversed; (b) inter-party consultation — agree on core principles even if details differ. 两路径：（a）法律固化——写入法律防轻易逆转；（b）跨党协商——细节可争，原则共识。
3
The 2025 decision to cancel consumer fuel charge by the new PM is a real example of what happens without institutional protection. 2025 年新总理取消消费者燃料碳费，是缺乏制度保护的实例。
Key sentence: "If the carbon price can be cancelled by the next election, it's not a policy — it's a temporary arrangement."金句：「若碳价可被下次选举一笔勾销，那就不是政策，只是临时安排。」
!
The new PM cancelled the consumer carbon tax in 2025. Does that mean the whole system failed? 2025 年新总理取消消费者碳税，是否等于整个体系失败？
›
2025 Cancel System Failure?
1
Not entirely — the industrial pricing system (OBPS) still exists. What was cancelled was the consumer-side fuel charge. 并非全部——工业侧定价（OBPS）仍在；取消的是消费者燃料费。
2
This actually proves our analysis: political sustainability is the weakest link in carbon pricing design. 反而印证我们分析：政治可持续性是碳定价设计最薄一环。
3
The benchmark page update in March 2025 shows policy is shifting, not disappearing — focus moved to industrial emissions. 2025 年 3 月基准线页面更新显示政策在调整而非消失——重心转向工业排放。
4
For companies, this creates exactly the kind of signal instability we discussed — shadow prices must be reassessed. 对企业而言正是我们讨论的信号不稳定——需重估影子价格。
Key sentence: "The system didn't fail — it adapted. But the adaptation itself created uncertainty, which is exactly the problem we analyze."金句：「体系未简单『失败』而是调整；调整本身带来不确定性——正是我们分析的问题。」
